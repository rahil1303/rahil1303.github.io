TA for **Fairness in Data Science** as part of the Creative Technology (CreaTe) bachelor's programme at the University of Twente. Supported students in developing critical thinking around data-based evidence, bias anticipation, and responsible design.

Guided students in quantifying uncertainty, interpreting data conclusions, and applying findings to design projects. Conducted exams and evaluation vivas.

**Institution:** University of Twente
**Period:** September 2025 – December 2025
