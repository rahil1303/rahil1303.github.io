TA for **Project Collective Intelligence** at VU Amsterdam — a project-based course on swarm intelligence, agent-based simulation, and collective dynamics.

Supported student teams building multi-agent simulations using the Violet framework. Guided weekly presentations and experiment design, and provided feedback on simulation results, collective behaviour analysis, and scientific reporting.

**Institution:** Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam
**Period:** May 2025 – August 2025
